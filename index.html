About Me

Hello everyone, let me introduce myself briefly.
My name is Flavio Forenza and I was born in Canosa di Puglia, but currently I live in the city of Padua in Veneto.
I'm a Computer Science Doctor and I'm expected to complete my master's degree at the University of Milan.

To date, I have carried out several projects, academic and otherwise.
One of my important projects concerns the development of an innovative automatic system capable of recognizing different cetaceans in the Gulf of Taranto.
The project was carried out entirely within the National Research Council (CNR) of Bari, in collaboration with the Jonian Dolphin Conservation.
